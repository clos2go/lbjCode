👨‍💻👨‍💻 Digital Citizenship Class Discussion👨‍💻👨‍💻

As we continue to explore and come to terms with our digital identities and profiles I would like for all of us to consider a couple of things. Our physical and virtual classroom community must be rooted in trust and respect and being kind and empathetic towards one another is the best way maintain this harmony. Digital citizenship is a process and takes mindful practice-- just like being a good human person in RL does. Digital citizen faux paux’s happen to the best of us and how you respond to your own bad behavior can say way more about you than the initial mistake. The goal in this activity is to begin processing how to prevent bad form on the internet and how to redeem yourself and make your way back to positivity when needed.

 In this discussion we will:

📌 Complete the online citizenship survey

📌Review Tips and tricks as whole class and with shoulder partners

📌Practice using sentence stems to respond to hypothetical scenarios with a partner

📌Complete the cyberbullying contract
