When posting online there are advantages and disadvantages. One advantage to Online vs Real Life is that online you can think through responses and be more creative before sharing. RL tends to put you on the spot and life can move fast if you’re not ready for it. Conversely, life moving fast can also be an advantage in RL. Sometimes our memories aren’t so good and people tend to forget most of the silly things we said or did in RL. Your online posts tho…. those are forever (digital footprint). The internet never forgets. 

That being said, knowing how to respond to sticky situations online is a very important skill to have. "Whatever!" or “It’s not my fault they are so sensitive!” in your default settings is lazy and does not fit our goal to always try to be good or better. To help us lead more inclusive and positive interactions online, which lead us towards growth while fostering our healthy community standard, take a look at the following sentence stems and practice using them with a partner in class.
