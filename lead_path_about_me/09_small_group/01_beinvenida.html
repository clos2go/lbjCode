09LP 👪 👨‍👩‍👧 SMALL GROUP COLLABORATION 👪 👨‍👩‍👧

1.  For this offline collaborative station, you will pick a partner (or more) and practice a “Bienvenida.” One person will ask the question on the left and the other partner (or partners will respond with an answer. Parts of the answer will be the same, but feel free to put whatever you want in there.

2.  After 2-5 minutes of practice, switch roles and complete the reflection sheet. 

Challenge yourself and Mix it up!

  • Pick 3 questions randomly and ask in any order.
  • Answer questions from a different point of view. Example: opposite day (answer the opposite of the real answer) or answer while role playing as someone else. 
  • Challenge yourself to insert your own detail words to all questions.
  • The phrase at the bottom is a sample of a possible way to end the interaction. Change it to a quote or some other phrase you love or know. Your phrase may be the phrase of the week and it may be putt up on the wall or door to add some positive energy! 

3.  Lastly, leave questions other could practice with in the discussion board down below.
